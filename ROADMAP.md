# Roadmap

This roadmap reflects current priorities and is subject to change based on iterative development. For detailed progress, see [Milestones](https://github.com/albdangarcia/animal-shelter/milestones).

## v1.1.0 — User & Admin Improvements
- User account settings page with soft-delete
- Assessment template management dashboard for admins
- Characteristics management dashboard for admins

## v1.2.0 — Enhanced Public Experience
- Pet listing filters by color, breed, and size

## v1.3.0 — Automation
- Automated medication task scheduler

## v1.4.0 — Volunteer & Financial Management
- Volunteer management and shift scheduling
- Donation and financial tracking system

## v1.5.0 — Communications & AI
- Centralized communications log for animals, adoptions, and individuals

## v1.6.0 - AI
- AI-powered activity log summaries with staleness detection
- AI-generated outcome packets for adoptions and transfers
- AI-powered task recommendations from assessment summaries

## Backlog/Ideas
